Benedetta (Bennie) Giocoli

I am a second-year MPhil student in Comparative Government at the Department of Politics and International Relations (DPIR), University of Oxford. My current research aims to identify the mechanisms of early political socialisation within the family using long-running household panel surveys from the United Kingdom. More broadly, I am interested in studying the formation of political identities, attitudes, and preferences using causal inference and machine learning methods. My studies at Oxford are fully funded by a DPIR Studentship. I also hold a Senior Scholarship at Lincoln College.

I previously obtained a First Class Honours BSc in Philosophy, Politics and Economics with Social Data Science from University College London.
